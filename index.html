About Me

My name is Emma Delzer, and I am a web and game developer born and raised in Riverside, California. In May 2016 I earned my Bachelor of Arts with a minor in LGBT Studies from the University of California Berkeley.

Technology, like other disciplines, is an influential force that has never been neutral in its applications in the world. I believe it is of utmost importance that we question: who controls the flow of information, who is allowed access to information, and who is marginalized within tech, and through tech applications.

"E pluribus unum."
